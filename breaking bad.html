About the Show

Breaking Bad is an American television series created by Vince Gilligan that aired on AMC from 2008 to 2013. The series stars Bryan Cranston as Walter White, a chemistry teacher which due to lung cancer diagnosis turned into a crime warlord. The observer may perceive the route through which Walter undergoes as he firstly just strive to ensure financial stability for his family but then gets corrupted and dragged into a violent criminal world alngoside with his former student Jessy Pinkmen with whom he strated cooking methaamphetamine at the beginning. Breaking Bad stars such personas as Anna Gunn, Dean Norris, RJ Mitte, Betsy Brandt, Giancarlo Esposito, Jonathan Banks, and Bob Odenkirk who became the main actor for TV-show spinoff of Breaking Bad called "Better Call Saul". The show received widespread critical acclaim and is considered one of the greatest TV shows of all time.

The viewership of Breaking Bad fluctuated during its five-season run:

  • Between Season 1 and Season 2, viewership decreased slightly by 0.11 million viewers (from 1.41M to 1.30M).
  • This was followed by steady growth:
    • Season 2 3: +0.22 million
    • Season 3 4: +0.38 million
  • The largest increase occurred between Season 4 and Season 5, with an impressive +2.42 million viewers (from 1.90M to 4.32M).

This significant growth reflects how popular the show has become during its final seasons. Increasing popularity was due to strong word of mouth, and high critical acclaim which led to Breaking Bad habving the fith in the history most watched final seasons of all time.
